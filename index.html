AI Awareness Middleware

The Next Evolution of AI

Most AI tools today are smart — but they're blind. They can't see your computer, your files, or what's changing unless you explicitly tell them.

This project is a proof of concept that gives AI something it's never really had before: awareness. I built a system where AI can:

  • See my files and folders
  • Read logs and notes
  • Monitor my system for changes
  • Remember context over time
  • Respond to me in plain English
  • Even notify me before I notice something has changed

It runs on a hybrid engine: a lightweight local model for speed and privacy, combined with a powerful cloud AI for higher-level interpretation. That means it works fast, offline when needed, and cost-effectively.

This isn’t just a better chatbot. It’s the start of a true digital assistant — one that can see, think, and act inside your real environment.

Disclaimer: This is a personal proof of concept built by one person in spare time. It uses a combination of open-source tools and self-developed middleware. It’s not a polished product, and I’m not offering support.
